Pizza in the garden#

We made some pizza. It was yummy. We ate it in the garden. Here is a picture of cooking the pizza.

In pizza dough, you have to use the following ingredients:

  • Flour
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Yeast

You can also add some olive oil to pizza dough. The dough for pizza can use commercial yeast, or be a sourdough.

Pizza is best cooked at a very high temperature.
